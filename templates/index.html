Yacht Brain

Welcome to the Starting page for Yacht Brain. If this is your first time launching this, please click here to create the initial database

Once you have setup the DB, click on Board/PIN Assignment on the left to tell us what Pins you are using on your RPI for your sensors.
For OneWire, be sure to connect to #4.
For Analog board(s), the recommended pins to use are: CLK = 18, MISO = 23, MOSI = 24, CS = 25.
You can define up to 2 Analog boards in the page.
This will also support MAX31855 Boards if you have those. These have an internal temperature on the board, and will connect to Pyro thermocouples for external reading.
